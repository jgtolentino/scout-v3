## 🎨 **UI/UX Design Principles**

### 🎯 **Visual Hierarchy**
1. **Header**: Brand + Navigation (Clean, minimal)
2. **Hero KPIs**: 6-card grid (Most important metrics)
3. **Main Charts**: Revenue trend + AI insights (Visual storytelling)
4. **Secondary Metrics**: Categories, regions, customers (Detailed analysis)
5. **Action Items**: Prioritized recommendations (Actionable insights)

### 📱 **Responsive Design**
- **Desktop**: 6-column KPI grid
- **Tablet**: 3-column KPI grid (2 rows)
- **Mobile**: 2-column KPI grid (3 rows)
- **Charts**: Adaptive sizing with touch interactions

### 🎨 **Color Coding**
- **Green**: Positive trends, targets met
- **Red**: Negative trends, alerts
- **Blue**: Primary actions, revenue metrics
- **Orange**: Warnings, opportunities
- **Gray**: Neutral data, background elements

### 🔧 **Interactive Elements**
- **KPI Cards**: Click for detailed drill-down
- **Charts**: Hover for values, click for filters
- **Category Segments**: Click to filter products
- **Regional Map**: Interactive geographic data
- **AI Insights**: Expandable recommendations

This wireframe represents the complete Scout Analytics MVP dashboard with all calculated values, interactive elements, and strategic insights for Philippine retail intelligence. 🚀
